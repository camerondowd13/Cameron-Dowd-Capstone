Why I'm building this

I've lived this problem at two very different companies.

At Oracle NetSuite — one of the largest SaaS companies in the world — the list-building grind was so brutal that reps would "steal" accounts the moment they fell out of someone else's name. People were prospecting on Thanksgiving and Christmas just to protect their own pipeline.

At Stampli, a startup, I hit the same wall in a different shape — leaner team, same problem: research time eating into the only hours that actually generate pipeline.

Both times, the issue wasn't effort. It was time allocation. Every hour spent finding a company and a reachable contact is an hour not spent talking to a buyer. That imbalance is what this tool is built to fix.

I'm not building this from a case study or a survey. I'm building it because I've paid for this problem with my own time — and I don't think anyone should have to work a holiday just to keep their pipeline safe.

— Cameron Dowd Account Executive, Stampli · Formerly Oracle NetSuite
