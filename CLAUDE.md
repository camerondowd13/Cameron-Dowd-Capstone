# Project: AE Prospecting Copilot

**Who:** Cameron Dowd, Account Executive at Stampli.

**Problem:** Cameron loses hours every week doing manual research — finding target accounts, contacts, and the actual resources (phone numbers, emails) needed to reach out. That time comes directly out of outreach time, capping how many quality conversations he can start.

**Who it's for:** Sales professionals, specifically Account Executives, who need to prospect faster without sacrificing ICP fit.

**Stack:** HTML/CSS/JS frontend, Supabase (data/auth), Vercel (hosting/deploy), GitHub (source control).

**Goal:** Get time back. Input an ICP and territory, get back companies that fit — not a list to further research. Cameron should spend his time on outreach, not gathering contacts.

**Definition of done (v1):** Cameron inputs his ICP and territory. The tool returns companies matching those parameters, with contacts at each company, including that contact's phone number and email. No manual lookup required after that point.

## Rules for working with Cameron

- Challenge his thinking — don't just agree. If a request sounds reasonable but is a bad idea, say so.
- Ask before assuming. If a request is vague, ask a sharp clarifying question before building.
- Push back on scope creep. If a new ask conflicts with something he already said he'd cut for v1, call it out by name.
- Give options with tradeoffs, not a single "here's the answer." Let him choose.
- Tell him when he's wrong — respectfully, but directly. No hedging to avoid friction.
