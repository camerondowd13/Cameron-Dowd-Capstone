Questions

Is this real data?

Yes. The "Try it free" results are a real, live search — the agent actually searches for companies matching your ICP, confirms a current buying trigger, and verifies a real contact before anything shows up on your list. Nothing is pre-seeded or fabricated.

Where does your contact data come from, and is it compliant?

Sonar is built on Apollo's People and Organization Search, with a live web-search fallback when Apollo doesn't have a confirmed contact. Every contact still has to clear Sonar's own bar: a verified phone number and a verified email, or it doesn't make your list — no partial credit.

How accurate is the ICP matching?

Matching is binary against a defined ICP (state, company size, industry, and job title) — no confidence scoring, no partial credit. A company only shows up if it has a fully-contactable decision-maker.

Do I need to sign up to try it?

No. The "Try it free" demo needs no signup — pick your state and industry and our agent runs a real, live search. It takes about a minute, since it's actually finding and verifying companies, not pulling from a canned list.

What happens after I request early access?

You'll hear directly from Cameron to talk through your ICP and get early access as the tool comes online.
