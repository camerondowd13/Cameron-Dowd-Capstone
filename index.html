Questions

Is this real data?

The samples on this site are seeded from real ICP criteria — territory, company size, industry, and title — so they're representative of live output, not a live data pull. The full tool is in active development.

How accurate is the ICP matching?

Matching is binary against a defined ICP (territory, company size, industry, and title) — no confidence scoring, no partial credit. A company only shows up if it has a fully-contactable decision-maker.

No. The "Try it free" demo runs instantly with no signup — pick your territory and industry and see sample matches right away.
