# Cameron Dowd Capstone

Cameron Dowd Capstone is an AI-powered prospecting assistant built for account executives and SDRs who need to move faster from research to real conversations. Instead of manually digging through LinkedIn, company websites, news, and CRM data to piece together account context, ICP fit, and buying signals, users get an instant, synthesized profile of any target account — key contacts, firmographic fit against their ideal customer profile, recent triggers (funding rounds, leadership changes, tech stack shifts, expansion news), and suggested talking points — all in the time it used to take to open a single tab.

By compressing hours of research into minutes, it gives sales teams more time and mental bandwidth to focus on what actually moves deals forward: personalized outreach and quality conversations with the right people, at the right time.

## Approach

The tool scrapes the internet for target companies and contacts, then synthesizes that data into ICP-fit scores, buying triggers, and talking points.
