# Strategy

**Track:** A — Building for others (business/service)

## What it does + who it's for

An AI prospecting engine that hands AEs and SDRs verified, ICP-fit companies with
fully-contactable decision-makers — so every hour goes to outreach, not list-building.

Built for inside sales professionals — AEs and SDRs — who self-source their own
outbound lists and need ICP-fit companies plus fully-contactable decision-makers,
without doing the manual research themselves.

**Headline:** "We find them. You close them."

## The ONE action a visitor should take

**CTA: "Try it free"**

Opens a lightweight, in-page interactive demo. Visitor selects from their ICP inputs
(territory, industry, target title) and instantly gets back 2–3 sample matches —
company + contact — pulled from a small, pre-seeded dataset (no live backend needed
for the demo). Results are clearly labeled as sample/demo data.

**Why this shape, not a form or a "book a call":**
- One CTA only — no split attention.
- Zero wait between action and payoff — a form-then-wait kills momentum.
- Buildable today (client-side JSON filtering, no live scraping engine required).
- Honest — labeled as sample results, not a live data pull.

**Seed dataset scope (~15–20 entries), built around the real ICP from PRD.md:**
- Industries: construction, manufacturing, healthcare
- Territories: NY, FL, VA
- Company size: 20–399 employees
- Target titles: CEO, CFO, CTO, AP Manager, Director of Finance

## What makes Cameron uniquely right to build this

Cameron has lived this exact problem at two very different companies:

- **Oracle NetSuite** — one of the largest SaaS companies in the world. The
  list-building/prospecting burden was so severe that reps would "steal" accounts
  that fell out of another rep's name over holidays — people were prospecting on
  Thanksgiving and Christmas to protect territory.
- **Stampli** — a startup, where he hit the same core problem in a different form
  (leaner team, same time-allocation squeeze between research and outreach).

This isn't a hypothetical pain point pulled from a survey — it's a problem he's
personally paid for in lost personal time at both a massive enterprise and a startup.
The mission, in his words: no one should have to prospect on a holiday to protect
their own pipeline.
