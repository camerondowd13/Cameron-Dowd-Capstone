Frequently asked questions

If something's not covered here, get in touch directly.

Is this real data?

Yes. The "Try it free" results are a real, live search — the agent actually searches for companies matching your ICP, confirms a current buying trigger, and verifies a real contact before anything shows up on your list. Nothing is pre-seeded or fabricated. The example board and dashboard sections below your results mix in illustrative sample accounts alongside your real leads (clearly flagged "live"), so you can see the full product at once — but your actual results are never faked.

Where does your contact data come from, and is it compliant?

Sonar is built on Apollo's People and Organization Search as the primary source, with a live web-search fallback (Claude + Exa) for companies or contacts Apollo doesn't have. Every contact still has to clear Sonar's own bar: a verified phone number and a verified email, or it doesn't make your list — no partial credit. When neither source can confirm a direct contact, you get the company's general office line instead of nothing.

How accurate is the ICP matching?

Matching is binary against a defined ICP (state, company size, industry, and job title) — no confidence scoring, no partial credit. A company only shows up if it has a fully-contactable decision-maker. Size and industry fit are computed in code from fixed rules, never self-reported by the AI.

Why do I sometimes get fewer than 3 leads?

Sonar never pads results to hit a target number — it only shows companies that actually clear every gate: real ICP fit, a real current buying trigger, and a real reachable contact. If fewer than 3 companies in your exact search clear all three, you'll see fewer than 3, honestly, rather than a filler result that doesn't actually qualify.

Does picking a specific job title (like CTO) change my results?

Yes — genuinely, not cosmetically. Picking a title narrows the search to that specific role, which can mean fewer fully-verified named contacts (some titles are rare or under-documented at certain company types), and a higher chance of falling back to the company's general office line instead of a named person. Leaving it on "Any job title" gives the agent the most room to find a fully-verified contact.

What industries does this support?

Construction, manufacturing, and healthcare — chosen as proxies for high invoice volume (200+ invoices/month), the actual underlying target that isn't directly searchable.

Do I need to sign up to try it?

No. The "Try it free" demo needs no signup — pick your state and industry and the agent runs a real, live search. It takes about a minute, since it's actually finding and verifying companies, not pulling from a canned list.

Is there a limit to how many times I can search?

Yes — a modest daily cap per visitor, since each live search costs real compute and API usage. If you hit it, you'll see a clear message rather than a silent failure, and it resets the next day.

What happens after I request early access?

You'll hear directly from Cameron to talk through your ICP and get early access as the tool comes online.
